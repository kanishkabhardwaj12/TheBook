About J-Book

Welcome to J-Book, a platform crafted exclusively for JIIT students to connect, share, and stay informed about campus happenings.

Here’s what J-Book offers:

  • Post updates and broadcasts about important campus events and news.
  • Follow other students to build connections within the Jaypee community.
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest events and announcements.

We’re thrilled to provide a space for interaction, engagement, and community-building among Jaypee students. Enjoy connecting!
